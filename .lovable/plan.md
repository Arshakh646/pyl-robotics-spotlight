

# PYL Robotics – Corporate Website

## Overview
A modern, professional corporate website for PYL Robotics showcasing solar panel cleaning solutions and custom robotics design services. The site will feature a futuristic, engineering-driven aesthetic with a dark blue, white, and grey palette accented by green/yellow energy tones.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- **Hero Section** with bold headline, tagline about solar cleaning robotics, and animated background visuals
- **Call-to-Action buttons**: "Get a Quote" and "Contact Sales"
- **Product highlights** – cards for Solar Cleaning Brush, Cleaning Robot, and Custom Robotics
- **Stats/trust bar** – key metrics (e.g., panels cleaned, energy saved, clients served)
- **Testimonials or client logos section**
- **Footer** with quick links, contact info, and social media

### 2. About Us
- Company story, vision, and mission statements
- Core values: innovation, sustainability, engineering excellence
- Team or leadership highlights
- Timeline or milestones section

### 3. Products Page
- **Solar Cleaning Brush** – features, benefits, use cases, and image gallery
- **Solar Panel Cleaning Robot** – technical specs, automation features, water efficiency, and image gallery
- Each product in a dedicated section with clear CTAs to request a quote

### 4. Services Page
- Custom robotics design process (Design → Prototype → Test → Deploy)
- Industries served and engineering capabilities
- Visual process flow/timeline

### 5. Why PYL Robotics
- Key differentiators presented as icon cards: efficiency, durability, innovation, cost savings, after-sales support
- Comparison or benefits overview

### 6. Industries & Applications
- Sections for rooftop solar, utility-scale solar farms, and industrial facilities
- Use case descriptions with relevant visuals

### 7. Contact Us
- Contact form with name, email, company, phone, and message fields (validated with Zod)
- Email display: sales@pylrobotics.com
- CTA buttons for quick inquiry
- Optional embedded map placeholder

---

## Design & UX
- **Color palette**: Dark navy blue (`#0F1B2D`), white, light grey, with green (`#22C55E`) and amber/yellow accents
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif fonts, bold headings, readable body text
- **Navigation**: Sticky top navbar with smooth scroll, mobile hamburger menu
- **Animations**: Subtle fade-in and slide-up effects on scroll
- **Responsive**: Fully mobile-friendly layout across all breakpoints
- **Footer**: Consistent across all pages with links, contact info, and copyright

## Technical Notes
- No backend needed – this is a static marketing/corporate site
- Contact form will be front-end only (can be connected to a backend later if needed)
- All content will use placeholder images that can be swapped with real product photos
